TOPE OJERINDE

Tope Ojerinde is a strong believer in relationships, emotional and mental well- being.
She hopes to spread across boundaries the message of 'Hope' in all ramifications. She has had her fair share
of personal struggles ranging from low self esteem, inappropriate management of people's opinions,
inability to maintain healthy relationships and insecurities. she overcame her challenges by developing
a deep relationship with God and applying all the lessons she acquired through her strugles into her
teachings and writings.
Tope graduated from TASUED has a master degree from the University of Lagos. She currently works as
a relationship coach, where she writes and teaches about relationships, emotional ad mental Stability.

She has a Whatsapp counselling session for teenagers struggling with low self-esteem and she hopes to
launch the Youtube session of it soon.
